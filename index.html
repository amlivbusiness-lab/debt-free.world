Why this exists

“I’m not an expert who studied financial hardship from the outside. I lived it — more than once.

I’m a mother of three, navigating the same messy, exhausting, beautiful chaos that millions of families know well. Over the years, I’ve lost around €45,000 through corporate fraud, broken contracts, and construction scams — not in one blow, but in the way these things usually happen: gradually, then all at once, each time thinking it couldn’t happen again.”

But somewhere alongside all of that, I kept showing up for the people around me. Not because I had extra resources. I didn’t. But because that’s just what you do — you show up.

I’ve spent years building community around me: running a charity event that has now reached over 3,000 people, creating spaces where kids could just be kids, supporting families in the quiet ways that don’t make headlines but matter deeply.

That’s the world I believe in. One where we figure it out together.

And this tool is part of that. I built it because honest, free, practical financial help shouldn’t be a privilege. It should just exist. For everyone — including people exactly like me, who worked hard, tried to do right, and still found themselves buried under debt they didn’t fully choose.

If that’s where you are right now, you’re not alone. And you don’t have to figure it out alone.”

Vilma Paasikivi
Founder, Debt-Free.world
Nokia, Finland · Mother of three
