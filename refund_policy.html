1. Why We Cannot Offer Automatic Refunds

Debt-Free.world generates personalized content immediately and uniquely for each user based on the specific financial data they provide. Your 90-day plan, AI-generated creditor letters, and all Premium content are created exclusively for your situation the moment you submit — they cannot be reversed, reassigned, or "returned" in any meaningful sense.

Legal basis: EU Directive 2011/83/EU on Consumer Rights, Article 16(m) — the right of withdrawal does not apply to digital content not supplied on a tangible medium where performance has begun with the consumer's prior express consent and the consumer has acknowledged losing their right of withdrawal. Finnish Consumer Protection Act (Kuluttajansuojalaki) Chapter 6, Section 15(13).

By confirming your purchase, you expressly consent to immediate delivery and acknowledge that the statutory 14-day right of withdrawal does not apply. This is confirmed by a clear checkbox at checkout — you will never be charged without explicitly acknowledging this.

2. Try Before You Buy

We strongly encourage every potential Premium customer to use the Free plan first. Your free 90-day plan is generated using the same AI, the same quality, the same care. Premium adds tools — it does not change the quality of the core plan. If you are not satisfied with the Free plan, do not purchase Premium.

3. Our Goodwill Commitment

While we are not legally obligated to offer refunds for immediately delivered digital content, we are committed to being fair. We review every dissatisfaction case individually.

We will issue a goodwill refund in these situations:

  • The service experienced a technical failure that prevented delivery
  • Your plan was not generated in the language you selected
  • The content was clearly incorrect due to a system error on our side
  • Other situations where we determine a refund is the fair outcome

To request a goodwill review, email support@debt-free.world with your purchase email and a brief description of the issue. We respond within 1 business day.

All paid plans are one-time payments. There are no subscriptions and nothing to cancel. Your access expires at the end of the period you paid for. You will never be charged automatically.

6. Service Discontinuation

If we discontinue a paid service while you have active access, we will issue a pro-rated refund for the unused portion, calculated to the day — regardless of the withdrawal waiver.
