Refund Policy

Debt-Free.world · Amliv Oy · Last updated: 2 June 2026

We want every customer to feel confident purchasing from Debt-Free.world. This Refund Policy explains your rights and how we handle refund requests.

1. Legal Position — EU Directive 2011/83/EU Art. 16(m)

Under EU Directive 2011/83/EU Article 16(m), consumers do not have a right of withdrawal for digital content that is not supplied on a tangible medium, where performance has begun with the consumer's prior express consent and acknowledgement that they thereby lose their right of withdrawal.

At the point of purchase, you expressly consented to the immediate delivery of your personalised digital content and acknowledged the loss of your right of withdrawal. This consent was recorded at checkout.

Therefore, Debt-Free.world is not legally obligated to provide refunds for completed purchases.

2. Our Goodwill Commitment

We do this together ✦

Despite the legal position above, we are committed to treating every customer fairly. We understand that financial stress is real, and we believe in the value of what we deliver. If you are genuinely unsatisfied, we will always listen and find a fair solution — because your trust matters more than any single transaction.

3. When We Will Issue a Refund

We will issue a full refund without question in the following situations:

  • You were charged but did not receive your access link or report within 24 hours
  • You were charged twice for the same purchase
  • A technical error prevented you from accessing your Premium Member Area
  • The content delivered was materially different from what was described

4. Case-by-Case Review

We review all other refund requests individually and with genuine care. While we cannot guarantee refunds outside the situations above, we commit to:

  • Responding to every refund request within 3 business days
  • Treating every case with fairness and human judgment
  • Offering alternative solutions where a full refund is not appropriate (e.g. extended access, personalised support)

5. How to Request a Refund

1
Email us at support@debt-free.world Include your email address used at purchase and a brief description of the issue. Subject line: "Refund request"
2
We respond within 3 business days We will review your case and respond with our decision or a request for more information.
3
Refund processed within 5–10 business days Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method via Stripe. Timing depends on your bank.

6. Community Access Program

If you genuinely cannot afford Debt Recovery but need help, you may apply to our Community Access Program — which provides free Premium access in exchange for feedback and participation in our development process. Contact us at support@debt-free.world with subject line "Community Access".

7. Dispute Resolution

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to escalate your complaint to:

  • Finnish Consumer Disputes Board: kuluttajariitalautakunta.fi
  • EU Online Dispute Resolution: ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr

These services are free of charge for consumers and available to all EU residents.
