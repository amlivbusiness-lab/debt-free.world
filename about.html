The full story

I am not a financial expert. I didn't study economics or work in banking. I built Debt-Free.world because I lived through the kind of financial collapse that changes you — and I came out the other side without a single tool that actually helped.

Over several years I lost around 45 000 € through failed business contracts, a construction scam, and a chain of setbacks I didn't see coming. Three children. No income. More debt than assets.

What broke me wasn't the money. It was the silence. Every service I found was either unaffordable, hopelessly generic, or built for people who already had their finances under control. Nobody talked to me like a real person. Nobody said: here is your exact situation, here are your actual options, and here is what you do today.

I spent months figuring it out on my own — reading, calling, waiting, filling in forms that led nowhere. I got through it. But I shouldn't have had to do it alone, and neither should you.

"I spent years helping thousands of other people through hard times. I never protected myself. When I finally needed help — I couldn't find it."

In 2025 I started building the thing I wish had existed. Not another article. Not a calculator with a phone number at the bottom. A real tool — one that takes your actual numbers, understands your actual situation, and gives you a real plan in your own language.

I wanted it to feel like talking to someone who has been there. Someone who doesn't judge the numbers you type in. Someone who says: okay, this is where you are — and here is how we move forward.

Debt-Free.world launched in 2026. It's free because that was never up for discussion. The people who need this most are the ones who can least afford to pay for it first.

— Vilma P

The bigger picture

We didn't build a debt app. We built something that stays with you. The same person who comes to us in crisis — overwhelmed, not knowing where to start — should be able to come back when they're ready to grow their income, build savings, or start a business. That's why Debt-Free.world is designed as an ecosystem, not a one-time tool.

We start with debt because that's where most people are when they find us. But this platform is built for the entire journey — from the worst month to the best decade.
