Free Debt Help by Country [2026] — Your Rights & Local Resources

Every country listed here has free, government-funded or nonprofit debt counseling available. Most people don't know it exists — or how to access it. This is the complete guide.

Finland

Free counseling

  • Takuu-Säätiö — Free debt counseling and guarantees for debt consolidation. takuu-saatio.fi
  • Municipal debt counseling (velkaneuvojat) — Free, available through your local social services office (sosiaalitoimisto). Book via your municipality's website.
  • KELA — Income support, housing allowance, and basic social assistance for those in financial hardship. kela.fi
  • Kuluttajaneuvonta — Consumer advisory service for disputes with creditors. Part of the Finnish Competition and Consumer Authority. kkv.fi

Legal options

  • Debt restructuring (yksityishenkilön velkajärjestely) — Formal court-supervised process. Requires inability to pay. Applied through district court. Takes 3–5 years to complete, after which remaining debt is discharged.
  • Payment arrangement with Ulosottovirasto — If debt has gone to enforcement, the bailiff's office can arrange a payment plan.
Your rights
Creditors cannot contact you outside reasonable hours. Debt collection fees are capped by law. You have the right to demand written confirmation of any debt before paying.
🇸🇪
Sweden

Free counseling

  • Kronofogden (Swedish Enforcement Authority) — Offers free debt restructuring (skuldsanering) and budget counseling referrals. kronofogden.se
  • Municipal budget and debt counselors (budget- och skuldrådgivare) — Free by law in every Swedish municipality. Find yours via your local commune website.
  • Konsumentverket — Consumer rights authority. Handles disputes with lenders and creditors. konsumentverket.se

Legal options

  • Skuldsanering — Swedish debt restructuring. Administered by Kronofogden. If approved, you pay what you can afford for 3 years, then remaining debt is discharged.
  • F-skuldsanering — For self-employed individuals with business debt.
Your rights
Creditors must follow Inkassolagen (Collection Act). Harassment is illegal. You can demand itemized debt statements at any time. Statute of limitations is generally 3 years for consumer debt.
🇩🇪
Germany

Free counseling

  • Caritas, Diakonie, AWO, Rotes Kreuz — All offer free Schuldnerberatung (debt counseling) across Germany. Search "Schuldnerberatung + [your city]" to find your nearest office.
  • Verbraucherzentrale — Consumer advice centers in every German state. Offer low-cost debt and financial counseling. verbraucherzentrale.de
  • Bundesarbeitsgemeinschaft Schuldnerberatung (BAG-SB) — National association that can help you find accredited free debt counselors. bag-sb.de

Legal options

  • Privatinsolvenz (consumer insolvency) — 3-year process. After completing it, remaining debt is discharged (Restschuldbefreiung). Requires attempt at out-of-court settlement first.
  • Pfändungsschutzkonto (P-Konto) — Protected bank account that shields a minimum income amount from creditor seizure. Every German bank must provide this on request.
Your rights
Debt collection must be conducted by licensed agencies. Harassment and false threats are illegal under § 4 BDSG and UWG. Consumer debt statute of limitations: 3 years from year-end.

United Kingdom

Free counseling

  • StepChange Debt Charity — UK's largest free debt advice charity. Full debt management plans, IVA referrals, bankruptcy guidance. stepchange.org · 0800 138 1111 (free)
  • National Debtline — Free debt advice by phone and online. Run by Money Advice Trust. nationaldebtline.org · 0808 808 4000 (free)
  • Citizens Advice — Free, independent advice including debt, benefits, and legal rights. citizensadvice.org.uk
  • MoneyHelper — Government-backed financial guidance. Debt tool and advisor locator. moneyhelper.org.uk

Legal options

  • Debt Relief Order (DRO) — For debts under £30,000. Freeze creditor action for 12 months; debt discharged if situation unchanged. Fee: £90.
  • Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA) — Formal agreement to pay portion of debt over 5–6 years. Remainder discharged.
  • Breathing Space (Debt Respite Scheme) — 60-day legal protection from creditor action while you get advice. Free to apply through a debt adviser.
Your rights
FCA rules prohibit creditor harassment. Consumer debts become statute-barred after 6 years (5 in Scotland) if no payment or written acknowledgment. You can request a full statement of account at any time.

Netherlands

Free counseling

  • Gemeentelijke schuldhulpverlening — Every Dutch municipality is legally required to offer free debt assistance. Apply via your gemeente (local authority).
  • Nibud — National Institute for Family Finance Information. Free tools, budgeting, and debt guidance. nibud.nl
  • Geldfit.nl — Government-backed financial health platform. Free advice and referrals. geldfit.nl

Legal options

  • WSNP (Wet schuldsanering natuurlijke personen) — Dutch debt restructuring act. 18-month process supervised by a bewindvoerder (administrator). Remaining debt discharged on completion.
Your rights
Incasso (collection) fees are legally capped. Creditors cannot add unreasonable costs. Debt statute of limitations: 5 years from due date.
🇫🇷
France

Free counseling

  • Banque de France — Commission de surendettement — Free official debt overindebtedness procedure. Apply at any Banque de France branch. banque-france.fr
  • UDAF (Union Départementale des Associations Familiales) — Free family financial counseling in every département.
  • CCAS (Centre Communal d'Action Sociale) — Municipal social action centers offering free financial advice.

Legal options

  • Procédure de surendettement — France's overindebtedness procedure via Banque de France. Can result in debt restructuring plan or, in severe cases, full discharge (rétablissement personnel).
Your rights
Consumer debt prescription period: 2 years from last payment or contact. Creditors cannot harass debtors — subject to criminal penalties under Article 222-33-2-2 of the Penal Code.

Norway

Free counseling

  • NAV (Arbeids- og velferdsetaten) — Norwegian welfare authority offers free economic counseling (økonomisk rådgivning). nav.no
  • Gjeldsoffer-Alliansen (GOA) — Debt victims' alliance. Free advice and support. goa.no

Legal options

  • Gjeldsordningsloven — Debt arrangement act. Court-supervised 5-year payment plan; remaining debt discharged. Requires application through NAV first.
Your rights
Consumer debt limitation period: 3 years. Creditors must follow the Inkassoloven strictly. Unreasonable fees are prohibited.
🇩🇰
Denmark

Free counseling

  • Gældsrådgivning Danmark — Free debt counseling service. gaeldsraadgivning.dk
  • Forbrugerrådet Tænk — Consumer council with free financial advice tools. taenk.dk
  • Kommunal gældsrådgivning — Most municipalities offer free debt counseling through social services.

Legal options

  • Gældsanering — Debt discharge procedure through the courts for those with no realistic repayment prospect. 5-year process.
Your rights
Debt limitation period: 3 years under Forældelseloven. Inkassoloven limits collection fees and prohibits harassment.
🇪🇸
Spain

Free counseling

  • ADICAE — Consumer and user association. Free financial and debt advice. adicae.net
  • Servicios sociales municipales — Municipal social services offices offer free debt counseling in most cities.
  • OCU (Organización de Consumidores y Usuarios) — Consumer organization. Free basic advice; paid membership for full service. ocu.org

Legal options

  • Ley de Segunda Oportunidad — Spain's second chance law (Ley 25/2015). Allows discharge of unsecured debt for individuals who meet criteria. Requires negotiation attempt first; administered through courts.
Your rights
Consumer debt prescription: 5 years under the Civil Code. The Ley Orgánica 3/2018 protects personal data related to debt. Creditors cannot list you in credit registers (like ASNEF) without formal notification.

Poland

Free counseling

  • UOKiK (Urząd Ochrony Konkurencji i Konsumentów) — Consumer protection office. Free advice line and complaint system. uokik.gov.pl
  • Rzecznicy Konsumentów — Municipal consumer ombudsmen (powiatowy rzecznik konsumentów) offer free debt and consumer advice in every poviat.
  • Federacja Konsumentów — Consumer federation. Free helpline: 800 007 707. federacja-konsumentow.org.pl

Legal options

  • Upadłość konsumencka — Consumer bankruptcy. Significantly reformed in 2020 — much more accessible now. Court process; debt can be discharged over 3–7 years.
Your rights
Debt limitation period: 3 years for most consumer debts, reduced from 10 years in 2018 reform. Debt collectors must identify themselves and provide full debt documentation on request.

EU-wide rights every debtor should know

Regardless of which EU country you live in, these protections apply:

  • Consumer Credit Directive (2023/2225/EU) — Creditors must provide clear pre-contractual information. Total cost of credit must be disclosed.
  • Unfair Commercial Practices Directive — Creditors and collectors cannot use misleading or aggressive practices. This includes false urgency, threats, and misrepresentation of legal status.
  • GDPR — Your debt data is personal data. Creditors and collection agencies must handle it lawfully. You have the right to access, correct, and in some circumstances delete debt-related records.
  • Statute of limitations — Every EU country has one. Once it expires (3–10 years depending on country), a creditor cannot sue to enforce the debt. Partial payment or written acknowledgment typically restarts the clock.
