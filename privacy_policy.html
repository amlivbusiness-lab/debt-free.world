2. Sensitive Financial Data

You voluntarily enter financial information (debts, income, expenses) to generate your personalised plan. This data is:

  • Processed immediately to generate your report
  • Transmitted securely via encrypted connection (HTTPS)
  • Processed by Anthropic's AI API (claude-sonnet model) under data processing agreement
  • Not sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes
  • Not linked to your identity beyond your email address

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate and deliver your personalised financial plan
  • To send you your Premium Member Area access link
  • To send you follow-up emails relevant to your financial journey (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our services through anonymised analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Data Retention

  • Email address: retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Financial data entered in forms: not stored beyond the session used to generate your report
  • Premium Member Area tokens: automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years as required by Finnish accounting law
  • Analytics data: retained per Google Analytics default settings (14 months)

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

Right of access Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Right to rectification Ask us to correct inaccurate data about you.
Right to erasure Ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
Right to restriction Ask us to limit how we process your data.
Right to portability Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
Right to object Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@debt-free.world. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi).

7. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics purposes (Google Analytics 4) with your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies in your browser. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, please contact us immediately at support@debt-free.world.
