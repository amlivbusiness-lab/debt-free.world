2. What Data We Collect

When you use Debt-Free.world, we collect:

  • Email address — to deliver your plan and, with consent, send relevant updates
  • Financial data you enter — income, debt amount, debt type, monthly costs, housing situation, employment status, household size, country, and interest rate
  • Language and goal preference — to generate your report correctly
  • Purchase data — for paid plans, processed by Stripe (we do not store payment card details)
  • Analytics data — if you consent to cookies, we collect anonymized usage data via Google Analytics 4 (pages visited, session duration, country). This data contains no personally identifiable information.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, social security numbers, or bank account details.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Service delivery — generating and emailing your personalized 90-day debt exit plan
  • Contact list — adding your email to our mailing list (only with your explicit consent)
  • Marketing — sending relevant updates and offers (only with your explicit consent)
  • Analytics — understanding how visitors use our site so we can improve it (only with cookie consent)
  • Legal obligations — accounting and VAT compliance under Finnish and EU law

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance — processing your data to generate and deliver your plan
  • Legal obligation — financial record-keeping under Finnish accounting law and EU VAT rules
  • Legitimate interest — improving our service
  • Consent — marketing communications, mailing list, and analytics cookies. Consent may be withdrawn at any time.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use two categories of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary — essential for the site to function (e.g. remembering your cookie preference). These do not require consent.
  • Analytics (Google Analytics 4) — anonymized data about how visitors use our site. No advertising. No cross-site tracking. Activated only with your explicit consent via our cookie banner.

You can accept or decline analytics cookies via the cookie banner when you first visit the site. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

We do not use advertising cookies, social media trackers, or any third-party marketing pixels.

6. Data Retention

  • Financial data entered in the form — not stored by us. Processed in real time, then discarded.
  • Email address — retained in Brevo until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Purchase records — retained for 6 years as required by Finnish accounting law (Kirjanpitolaki 2:10 §)
  • Marketing consent records — retained for 3 years for legal compliance
  • Analytics data — retained in Google Analytics for 14 months (default setting)

  • Brevo / Sendinblue (France/EU) — email delivery and contact management. GDPR-compliant.
  • Stripe (USA/EU) — payment processing. PCI-DSS compliant. We never see or store your card details.
  • Cloudflare (USA) — API proxy. Processes requests in transit only. EU SCCs apply.
  • Google Analytics 4 (USA) — anonymized analytics, activated only with cookie consent. EU SCCs apply. IP anonymization enabled.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers.
