Amliv Oy operates Debt-Free.world. We are the data controller for all personal data processed in connection with this service.

What data we collect and why

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service you have requested.

Free plan users

When you submit the free debt recovery form, we collect:

  • Email address — to deliver your 90-day plan
  • Financial information you enter (income, debt amounts, household type, country, employment situation, housing costs) — to generate your personalised plan
  • Your selected language and country — to localise your plan

Free plan data storage: Financial data entered in the free plan form is used solely to generate your report. It is transmitted to our AI provider (Anthropic) for processing and is not intentionally retained by Amliv Oy after the report generation process has been completed. Your email address is stored in our email system (Brevo) to deliver your plan and send relevant updates.

Note: like all internet services, our infrastructure providers (Cloudflare) may retain technical request logs for short periods for security and operational purposes. These logs do not contain your financial form data.

Premium Member Area users

When you purchase a Premium membership and complete the onboarding form, we collect and store:

  • Email address
  • First name
  • Country and language
  • Financial information you provide: debt total range, employment situation, household type, housing situation, income range, savings buffer, collections or enforcement status, primary barrier to debt repayment
  • Your AI-generated personalised plan
  • Stripe payment metadata (transaction ID, payment date — we do not store card details)

How and why Premium data is stored: This data is stored in Cloudflare Workers KV (a key-value database) associated with your secure access token. The primary purpose is to power your personalised Premium Member Area and display your plan on subsequent visits during your membership period. Data is retained for the duration of your purchased membership tier and then automatically deleted.

Providing this financial information is voluntary. You may access your Member Area without completing the onboarding form, but your personalised plan will not be generated.

Membership periods and data retention

Premium membership access and associated data retention periods vary by purchased plan:

  • Debt Recovery (90 days) — member data retained for 90 days from date of purchase
  • Income Builder Starter (90 days) — member data retained for 90 days from date of purchase
  • Income Builder Pro (180 days) — member data retained for 180 days from date of purchase
  • Wealth Builder / Business Builder (365 days) — member data retained for 365 days from date of purchase

After the applicable retention period, personal identifiers are deleted from our systems. We may retain fully anonymised statistical data derived from your submission indefinitely for research, reporting, impact measurement and service improvement purposes (see the Research and impact measurement section below).

Other data retention periods:

  • Free plan email address — retained in Brevo until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Payment records — retained as required by Finnish accounting law (7 years)

Research and impact measurement

Part of the mission of Debt-Free.world is to demonstrate that accessible financial guidance creates measurable, real-world change. To support this mission, we may use data derived from user submissions for the following additional purposes:

  • Statistical research and analysis of debt patterns, recovery trajectories, and outcomes
  • Impact measurement and reporting (e.g. “percentage of users who reported becoming debt-free within 12 months”)
  • Service improvement and product development
  • Grant applications, funding proposals, and social impact reporting
  • Academic or non-profit collaboration on financial wellbeing research

How we protect you in research use: Any data used for the purposes above will be aggregated, anonymised or pseudonymised before use — meaning it cannot reasonably be used to identify you as an individual. We will not share identifiable personal data with researchers or funders. We may retain fully anonymised statistical records indefinitely for these purposes, even after your personal data has been deleted.

This use of anonymised data does not require additional consent under GDPR because it does not involve processing of personal data. We disclose it here in the interest of full transparency.

AI processing and Anthropic

Your personalised plan is generated by Claude, an AI model developed by Anthropic. When you submit financial information, it is sent to Anthropic's API to generate your plan.

Anthropic processes this data as a data processor acting on our behalf. We use Anthropic's API under their standard commercial terms. Anthropic's standard API does not use submitted data to train AI models. We recommend reviewing Anthropic's Privacy Policy and their usage policies for their current data handling practices.

Your financial data is transmitted to Anthropic only for the purpose of generating your plan. It is not shared with Anthropic for any other purpose.

Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under GDPR:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) — processing necessary to deliver the service you have purchased or requested
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — sending relevant updates, measuring service impact, and improving the service, where these interests are not overridden by your rights
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — retaining payment records as required by Finnish accounting law
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — where you have explicitly agreed, such as for marketing communications; you may withdraw consent at any time

Who we share your data with

We use the following service providers to operate Debt-Free.world. Each processes data only as necessary to provide their service:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI processing to generate your personalised plan. See the AI processing section above.
  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — email delivery and contact list management. Your email address is stored in Brevo to send your plan and relevant updates.
  • Cloudflare — infrastructure, DNS, security and Workers KV storage for Premium member data. Cloudflare may process request metadata in the normal course of providing network and security services.
  • Stripe — payment processing. We share only what Stripe requires to process your payment. Stripe is an independent data controller for payment data. See Stripe’s Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share identifiable personal data with advertisers or any third party not listed above.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Portability — receive a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, email support@debt-free.world with the subject line “Data request” and the email address associated with your account. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi) or the supervisory authority in your country of residence.

Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 is loaded only after you consent via the cookie banner displayed on your first visit. We use anonymised IP addresses and do not use cross-site tracking or advertising cookies.

If you decline analytics, no tracking scripts are loaded. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and declining again on your next visit.

Data security

We take the following technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Premium member tokens are cryptographically generated with 256-bit entropy and validated server-side on every request
  • Member tokens are stored in sessionStorage (not cookies or localStorage) and cleared from URLs immediately after validation to prevent exposure in browser history or analytics
  • Financial data is not stored in browser storage or transmitted in page URLs
  • Access to production infrastructure is restricted
  • Premium member data is automatically deleted after the applicable membership period
  • All API endpoints are rate-limited and restricted to authorised origins

International data transfers

Some of our service providers (Anthropic, Cloudflare, Stripe) operate infrastructure outside the European Economic Area. Where personal data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or the provider’s adequacy mechanisms as required by GDPR Chapter V. For further information, refer to the privacy policies of each provider linked above.

Children

Debt-Free.world is not directed at persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted personal data through our service, please contact us at support@debt-free.world and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when our data practices change. Material changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated date. For existing users, material changes will be communicated by email where we hold your email address. Continued use of the service after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
